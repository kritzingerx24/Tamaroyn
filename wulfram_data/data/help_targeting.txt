<P><P><P>
Targeting Commands
<BR>
------------------
<P>
t :              Target enemy tanks (if in range)
<br>
alt-t:			 Target friendly tanks
<P>
TAB:             Target objects in your view
<P>
u :              Target object under your reticle
<BR>
` :              Clear your target
<P>
f :              Target friendly refuel pad
<BR>
alt-f:			 Target hostile refuel pad (if in range)
<P>
r :              Target friendly repair pad
<BR>
alt-r:			 Target hostile repaid pad (if in range)
<P>
g :				 Target friendly energy unit
<BR>
alt-g:			 Target hostile energy unit (if in range)
<p>
F3:              Change radar range between short and long
<P>
Ctrl-F5:         Store current target in key F5
<BR>
Ctrl-F6:         Store current target in key F6
<BR>
Ctrl-F7:         Store current target in key F7
<BR>
Ctrl-F8:         Store current target in key F8
<P>
F5:              Recall target from key F5
<BR>
F6:              Recall target from key F6
<BR>
F7:              Recall target from key F7
<BR>
F8:              Recall target from key F8
<BR>
