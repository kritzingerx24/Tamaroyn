Twists photons into their left-hand-universe counterparts, thus oozing a darkness that obscures units and renders them invisible to radar.

The ability to throw around left-handed photons has been a child's toy for centuries, but it is only during the past fiew decades that the darklight has found practical utility in battle, due to a breakthrough in photon-handling precision: now, by carefully modulating the energy levels of emitted photons, the darklight is able to pulse in a cryptographically secure sequence that can be filtered by friendly forces, allowing them to see almost normally.

Since it primarily absorbs and re-emits existing light, the darklight does not require external power.
