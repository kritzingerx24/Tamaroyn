<P><P><P>
Navigation Commands
<BR>
-------------------
<P>
w:            Forward
<BR>
s:            Backward
<BR>
a:            Slide Left
<BR>
d:            Slide Right
<P>
arrow keys:   Forward, backward, turn left, turn right
<P>

q:            Raise hovering altitude
<BR>
a:            Lower hovering altitude
<P>

m:            Go to map screen
<BR>
n:            Cycle between visual, altitude, slope maps
<P>
j:            Activate jumpjets (tank only)
<P>
Numpad 0:     Set thrust to 0
<BR>
Numpad 1-9:   Set forward thrusters to various speeds
<P>
