<P><P><P>
Miscellaneous Commands
<BR>
----------------------
<P>
Insert/Delete:           Zoom in/out (useful for sniping)
<BR>
Shift-Insert:            Zoom in pretty far
<BR>
Shift-Delete:            Zoom out all the way
<P>

ENTER:                   Send a message
Page Up:                 Scroll message text upward
<BR>
Page Down:               Scroll message text downward
<P>

F2:                      Cycle between different camera views
<BR>

, :                      Drop cargo
<BR>
. :                      Deploy cargo
Pause:                   Status report
<BR>
Shift-Pause:             Distress call
<P>

Escape:                  Main menu
<BR>
P:                       Show player list
<BR>
Alt-P:                   Show your team's carrying list
<BR>
